Welcome to the Missing Persons Ghana website. We are the national point of contact for all missing persons and unidentified body investigations. We provide support and advice to police forces in order to resolve cases and act as a hub for the exchange of information and expertise in this area. We also maintain the national database of missing and unidentified records.

Report a Missing Person

If you believe that someone is missing then please contact your local police station. If you are extremely concerned for their welfare then dial 18555 Toll Free and ask for the Police or send an email to info@police.gov.gh.
